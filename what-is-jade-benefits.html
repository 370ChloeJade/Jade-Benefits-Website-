What Is Jade Benefits?

By Jade Benefits | Fort Lauderdale, FL | Updated 2026

If you have landed here, you are probably wondering exactly who we are and how we can help. The short answer: Jade Benefits is a health insurance brokerage based in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, and we exist to take the confusion out of getting covered. We work for you, not for a single insurance company, which means we compare plans across top rated carriers and help you land on the one that actually fits your life and budget, at no cost to you.

Who we are

Jade Benefits is a licensed, independent health insurance brokerage headquartered in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, serving clients with nationwide carrier networks. Because we are independent, we are not tied to any single insurer. Our job is to sit on your side of the table, understand your situation, and match you with coverage from A rated carriers such as UnitedHealthcare, Cigna, Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Humana, and Florida Blue.

What we do

We make getting the right health coverage simple from start to finish. That means:

  • Reviewing your needs, your doctors, your prescriptions, and your budget.
  • Comparing plans side by side across multiple top carriers.
  • Checking every subsidy and tax credit you may qualify for so you never overpay.
  • Handling the enrollment paperwork so nothing falls through the cracks.
  • Being there at renewal time and whenever questions come up.

Who we serve

We specialize in the people and businesses that are often underserved by big insurers:

  • Small groups and businesses setting up group coverage for their teams.
  • Self-employed and 1099 workers who navigate the marketplace on their own.
  • Families and individuals looking for the right balance of coverage and cost.
  • Trade industries such as construction, HVAC, and roofing crews.
  • Medical groups and professional services that need tailored group plans.
  • Truckers and owner operators who need coverage that travels nationwide.

In one sentence: Jade Benefits helps small groups, business owners, the self-employed, families, and truckers find and enroll in the right health plan, backed by nationwide networks and personal support.

What makes us different

Plenty of places will sell you a policy. What sets us apart is that we actually pick up the phone, explain your options in plain English, and never push a plan that does not fit. We are local to South Florida but work nationwide, we specialize in the hard to cover situations other agents avoid, and our clients consistently tell us the process felt honest, fast, and pressure free. That is reflected in our 5.0 rating on Google.

Why our help is free

A common question is how we can offer all of this at no charge. The answer is straightforward: as a licensed brokerage, we are paid by the insurance carriers, not by you. The premium you pay is the same whether you enroll on your own or work with us, so there is no downside to having an expert in your corner. You get personalized guidance, plan comparisons, and ongoing support for free.

How to get started

Getting started takes just a few minutes. Request a free quote online or call us, tell us a little about your situation, and we will compare your best options and walk you through them. There is no obligation and no pressure, just clear answers and a plan that fits.
